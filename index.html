Academic & Professional Profile

Blending complex system frameworks with practical mental diagnostics and organizational intelligence.

I specialize in developing machine learning systems, psycho-social risk management methods, and computational social science designs. My ultimate research goal is to understand human behaviors through networked systems.

As a faculty member at the Olin Business School, Washington University in St. Louis (WashU) and the co-founder of dnb analytics / COBSMIND AI, I aim to implement human-centered AI that provides explainable insights to solve business and social crises.

I am a four-time recipient of the Olin Business School's **Reid Teaching Award** (2021, 2022, 2023, 2024), and was honored with the **2024 Olin Research Award** for emergency resource optimization studies. I also serve as a grant reviewer for the National Science Foundation (NSF) and a book editor for Elsevier.
